The Problem
01

Traditional systems are built to detect visible objects.

Modern threats are designed to avoid detection.

Camouflage, environmental blending, and signal suppression reduce visibility — but they do not eliminate presence.

The limitation is not visibility.

It is expectation.

The Idea

PARALLAX approaches detection differently.

Instead of asking "Is something visible?"

It asks:

Is something present that should not be there?

Even concealed objects disturb their surroundings. These disturbances may appear as:

01
Subtle visual inconsistencies
02
Minor thermal variations
03
Small disruptions in RF signals

Individually insignificant.

Collectively meaningful.

System Overview

PARALLAX is structured as a layered system:

Sensors
Processing
AI Analysis
Fusion
Output
Multi-sensor data is captured from the environment
Signals are cleaned and synchronized
AI models learn normal environmental patterns
Deviations are detected as anomalies
Outputs are combined to improve reliability

The result is a probabilistic indication of abnormal presence.
